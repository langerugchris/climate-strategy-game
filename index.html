Chancellor's Briefing

From: Cabinet Office Permanent Secretary
Classification: OFFICIAL — For Minister's Eyes Only

Minister, your advisors have flagged three systemic risks that have derailed previous climate programmes:

  • 🔋 Grid stability — every wind farm you build needs backup storage. Without it, blackouts follow and the public turns against the whole programme
  • 🤝 Public consent — the transition raises energy bills. Workers in oil & gas face redundancy. Ignore the social cost and you lose the political mandate to continue
  • 🌡️ Delay is compounding — above 1.5°C, tipping points accelerate warming beyond your control. Early action is far cheaper than late action

Each term covers five years and £100bn to allocate across six investment areas. The trade-offs are real — you cannot do everything at once.
